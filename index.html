Journey of finding a passion

  • 1998 Google is born!

    And I was also born this year.
  • 2012 I took my first baby steps into the world of programming and social media when I made a tumblr blog for a show I really liked.
  • 2013 I was lucky enough to be able to participate in an exclusive writer's course. Which means I learnt a lot, wrote a short story, and now I'm a published author.
  • 2015 It happened again!
    While playing handball at the Oure Sport & Performing Arts boarding school, I ended up with another published work!

    I was also convinced I wanted to be a veterinarian. Or a film director.
  • 2018 I graduated Aarhus Statsgymnasium after three years of biochemistry and creative burnout.

    So now I knew I didn't want to be a veterinarian.
  • 2018 My gap year. I was reunited with my creativity during a roadtrip on the Westcoast of USA.

    I took a film director course, tried to learn what Python is but decided I should start by learning what programming actually is first.
  • 2020 Did I mention, I'm a bit of a geek? My friend and I hosted an online global event with over 40 participants for a fandom, and I had the responibility of design, SoMe, and blog maintanence.

    It was the point of no return; I had tip-toed the edge before, but now I was plummeting down the web design rabbit hole.
  • 2020 It got me! I started as a Multimedia Design student at Aarhus Business Academy, so I could expand my knowledge of design, learn more about programming, and last, but definitely not least, challenge my creativity.
  • 2022 I will graduate with a big smile! :D
